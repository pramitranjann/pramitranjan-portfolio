Red Wipe — How Much Red?

Same sweeping mechanic, different amounts of red. Each loops automatically.

Black panel sweeps across with a 3px red line at the front. Subtle nod to the palette.
B — FLASH + DARK WIPE
Quick red flash like a shutter firing, then a dark panel wipes through.
C — PURE DARK WIPE
Clean dark panel, no red at all. Fast and editorial without the punch.
